# Project Roadmap

This repository currently contains a minimal prototype of the Congruity Logistics (CL 1.0) metric.

Future development directions include:

## Short term

- Larger logistics datasets
- Additional simulation scenarios
- Visualization of CL evolution over time
- Sensitivity analysis of metric components (E, I, S)

## Medium term

- Integration with routing optimization models
- Comparative experiments with classical logistics efficiency metrics
- Extended notebooks and reproducible experiments

## Long term

- Application to real-world logistics systems
- Extensions of the Congruity metric to other complex flow systems
- Energy networks
- Mobility systems
- AI infrastructure systems
